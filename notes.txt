App-V package id is same for all computers, just run commands to install:
Mount-AppvClientPackage -PackageId packageid -VersionId versionid
This downloads the package.
ECLIPSE-PACKAGEID: C87BE6C9-9379-431A-AE70-FD15E8F1AACA
ECLIPSE-VERSIONID: BBA5E63B-FA99-4CF9-9FA8-1991602E58BC
